二、阅读理解（共1题；共10分）

5. （10分）阅读短文，判断正误。
    Mike has (有) a yellow pen. John has a black pencil box. Chen Jie has a blue ruler. Wu Yifan has a white flower. Bai Ling has a red bag.
(1)、Mike has a yellow pen.
(2)、 John has a green pencil box.
(3)、Chen Jie has a blue ruler.
(4)、 Wu Yifan has a brown flower.
(5)、Bai Ling has a red bag.
